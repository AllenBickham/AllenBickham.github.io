About Thai Food

While Thai food has a reputation for being spicy, Thai food is actually based on balance between different flavors including spicy, sour, sweet, salty, and bitter. While some Thai restuarants specialize in specific dishes, most have a huge menu of Thai and western fare and prepare Thai food from throughout the kingdom.
